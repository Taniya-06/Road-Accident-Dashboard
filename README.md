## **Road Accident Dashboard**

This interactive **Road Accident Dashboard** provides a comprehensive overview of road accident data to help identify trends, risk factors, and focus areas for safety improvements. Built using **Microsoft Excel**, it visualizes key metrics such as total casualties, accident severity, vehicle type, road type, and environmental conditions.

### **Key Insights**

1. **Total Casualties:**
   The dataset reports a total of **417,883 casualties**, highlighting the significant scale of road safety challenges.

2. **Severity Distribution:**

   * **Fatal Casualties:** 7,135 (≈1.7%)
   * **Serious Casualties:** 59,312 (≈14.2%)
   * **Slight Casualties:** 351,436 (≈84.1%)
     Most incidents result in minor injuries, but the number of serious and fatal accidents still requires attention.

3. **Casualties by Vehicle Type:**

   * **Cars:** 333,485 casualties (≈79.8%)
   * **Motorcycles:** 33,348
   * **Pedal Cycles:** 33,672
   * **Buses:** 12,798
   * **Agricultural Vehicles:** 1,032
   * **Others:** 3,424
     Cars contribute the highest proportion, suggesting the need for stricter car-related safety measures.

4. **Casualties by Road Type:**

   * **Single Carriageway Roads:** 309.7K casualties
   * **Dual Carriageway:** 67.4K
   * **Roundabouts & Slip Roads:** Minor shares
     Single carriageways remain the most accident-prone road type.

5. **Casualties by Road Surface:**

   * **Dry Roads:** 279,445 casualties
   * **Wet Roads:** 115,261 casualties
   * **Snow/Ice:** Minor percentage
     Accidents on dry surfaces indicate behavioral factors rather than weather conditions.

6. **Casualties by Light Condition:**

   * **Daylight:** 305K casualties
   * **Dark:** 112.9K casualties
     Daytime accidents dominate, suggesting high daytime traffic and possibly distracted driving.

7. **Urban vs Rural Trends:**

   * **Urban Areas:** 255.9K casualties
   * **Rural Areas:** 162K casualties
     Urban regions report higher accident frequency, likely due to denser traffic.

8. **Yearly Trend (2021–2022):**
   Monthly trends show a steady pattern, with minor fluctuations, indicating consistent accident frequency across the year.

### **Conclusion**

This dashboard highlights the importance of improving **urban traffic management**, **driver awareness**, and **road infrastructure**. The insights derived can support policymakers, law enforcement, and transport authorities in prioritizing **safety initiatives** and **resource allocation**.

### **Tools Used**

* **Microsoft Excel** for data cleaning, visualization, and dashboard design.
* **Slicers and Filters** for interactive analysis across years, road types, and regions.
